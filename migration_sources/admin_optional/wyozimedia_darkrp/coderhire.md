DarkRP Media Center is an extension of Media Center. It extends Media Center by adding projector, TV and radio entities  and couple of jobs to go with them.

##Features

---

**Optimized for DarkRP** All the videos play locally and automatically get destroyed when you're far enough from the video source to save bandwidth.

**Comes with three different entities** [a projector](http://puu.sh/4ihPl.jpg), [a television](http://puu.sh/4ihXP.jpg) and [a radio](http://puu.sh/4iibB.jpg).

**Comes with Cinema Owner and DJ jobs**

**rp\_downtown\_v4c\_v2 and rp\_downtown\_v4c\_v3 integration built-in** [Screenshot](http://puu.sh/4ieiv.jpg)

**Based on Media Center** so you can use all the same features and keep using your existing video library.

**Is a sandbox!** The possibilities are endless. Want to add a static camera that only one job can use? No problem!


## Installation

---

Just unzip the addon to your addons folder and you're good to go! Note: you also need the base Media Center installed for the addon to work properly.
