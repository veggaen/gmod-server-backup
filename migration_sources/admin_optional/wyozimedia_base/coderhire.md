Media Center allows you to play videos from various video hosting websites on your server. At the moment it supports Youtube, Vimeo, Soundcloud and MP3/OGG files.

##Features

---

**Quick video search** Allows you to quickly find a video.

![image be here](http://puu.sh/4id8w.png)

**Persistent storage** Videos stay over server restarts or crashes. 

**Youtube keyword search**  Allows adding videos you want extremely rapidly without copying and pasting links.

**Permission system**  Each one of the commands can be enabled or disabled for specific group.

**Admin mod independent** Should work with all major admin mods.
